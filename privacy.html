Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Desktop App

The PitchShow desktop application is open source (Apache 2.0). When you use your own API key, all AI generation happens directly between your machine and your chosen model provider. PitchShow does not receive, log, or store your prompts, presentation content, or API keys.

Cloud Service

When you use PitchShow Cloud (web app with hosted AI), we process your generation requests through our servers to route to AI providers. We collect:

  • Account information (email address)
  • Generation metadata (timestamps, model used, token counts) — not content
  • Hosted show files you choose to publish
  • Usage analytics (anonymous, aggregate)

We do not sell your data. We do not train AI models on your content. Generation requests are forwarded to AI providers (Anthropic, etc.) under their respective privacy policies.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your account settings. Hosted shows are removed immediately on deletion request.
