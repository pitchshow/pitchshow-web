Visual Styles

9 visual systems.
One click to switch.

Each style is a complete design language — colors, typography, animation timing, layout rhythm. Apply any of them to any deck at any point, without losing a word of content.

Tech / Sci-fi

Dark backgrounds, indigo-neon accents, grid lines, floating orbs. Typography is sharp and geometric. Animations are crisp and technical — snap-in rather than drift.

Best for
SaaS product launches AI / developer tools Technical demos Series A / B pitches
Business / Corporate

Structured grid, brand-consistent gold accents, executive typography. Animations are deliberate and professional — nothing flashy, everything intentional. Imports your brand colors automatically.

Best for
Board presentations Investor updates Sales proposals Quarterly reviews
Minimal / Pure

Near-white background, monochrome palette, maximum whitespace. Near-zero animation — content speaks without decoration. Typography-first, with tight leading and careful hierarchy.

Best for
Design agency pitches High-stakes proposals When the data is the story Minimalist brands
Fashion / Magazine

Black canvas, pink-magenta accents, bold editorial typography, dramatic contrast. Content enters with cinematic reveals. Large whitespace makes every element feel considered.

Best for
Creative agencies Lifestyle brands Portfolio presentations Brand strategy pitches
Academic / Research

Evidence-first layout with structured citation placement. Data density is high, visual decoration is low. Animations are subtle fades — content authority matters more than visual impact.

Best for
Conference presentations Research findings Policy briefings PhD defenses
Warm / Personal

Earth tones, human-scale typography, spring-physics animations that feel organic rather than mechanical. Made for stories — the kind of deck where the person behind it matters as much as the slides.

Best for
Founder stories Angel fundraising Personal narratives Community talks
Education

Progressive visual hierarchy that builds concept on concept. Clear numbered structure, step-by-step reveal animations. Designed for the learner's journey — each new element appears when needed, not all at once.

Best for
Training sessions Onboarding decks Course introductions Workshop facilitation
Tech Evangelism

Benefits-first storytelling with an energetic, community-optimized layout. Purple spectrum, bold reveal animations, slide-in effects that keep momentum through a long talk. Built for rooms full of people who want to be convinced, not just informed.

Best for
Conference keynotes Developer advocates Open source launches Hackathon pitches
Brand Custom

How it works

Change the style. Keep the content.

Styles are applied as a complete transformation — every slide updates at once. The narrative, the data, the speaker notes: all unchanged. Only the visual language switches.

Switch at any time

Apply a different style on slide 1 or slide 12. Nothing is locked in until you export. Try Tech/Sci-fi, hate it, switch to Minimal — the deck takes 2 seconds to re-render.

Brand Kit overrides any style

Import your brand file and your colors and fonts take precedence over the chosen style's defaults. You get the layout and animation language of the style with your own visual identity on top.

Vibe Mode still works

Switching styles doesn't affect Vibe Mode. You can still click any element and refine it after applying a style — the two systems are independent layers on the same source.

Pick one. Change your mind later.

The right style for your deck is the one that matches your audience and context. If you're not sure, start with Minimal — it's never wrong.
