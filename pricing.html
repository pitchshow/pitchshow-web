Pricing questions

Generation continues automatically — PitchShow switches to our fallback AI without interrupting your work. You'll see a subtle indicator in the UI. Your existing hosted shows, exported files, and projects are never affected. To get more Sonnet, either wait for next month's reset or upgrade to annual (your quota doubles from 5 to 10 decks per month).

Claude Haiku 4.5 is fast and capable — it handles everyday presentations, research, and iterations perfectly well. Most decks in most situations will be excellent with Haiku. Claude Sonnet 4.5 produces noticeably higher narrative quality: tighter structure, more persuasive English, better pitch storytelling. Use Sonnet for your most important presentations — investor pitches, major client proposals, and keynote talks. Save Haiku for drafts and routine work.

The $5 pass gives you one complete deck generation with Claude Haiku, one Animated PPTX export, and a hosted show URL that stays live for 30 days. It's designed for people with a one-off need — a pitch tomorrow, a presentation for a job interview, a single client proposal. No subscription, no renewal, no commitment.

Annual gives you two concrete advantages over monthly: (1) Sonnet quota doubles — 10 decks per month instead of 5. (2) Brand Kit — PitchShow remembers your brand colors, fonts, logo, and communication style across every deck you make. By your 10th deck, the AI generates content that sounds and looks like it was written by someone who knows your company inside out. Brand Kit compounds over time, making the product more valuable the longer you use it. Plus you save 31% and lock in today's pricing.
